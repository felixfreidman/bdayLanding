We have it in our hands.

The past year has challenged us all and more or less pushed us out of our comfort zone. It showed us that waiting won't get us anywhere. We have to stick with it, approach things in a new way and rethink things.

We need creative solutions and must not forget to celebrate and enjoy our successes.

The story of how our beer came about is emblematic of this.

scroll

Clou turned ten years old in 2020. This should be celebrated – with a big party and home-brewed beer.

We wanted to say thank you and toast to the partnerships and friendships that have been created. You can imagine why it never happened. And so we waited for better times with 200 liters of beer in the cellar.

It took us a long time to accept that our celebration would probably no longer work and that we would have to find another way to convey our appreciation to you.

Clou is now 11 years old. But that's not the point anymore.

Our anniversary year has taught us to accept, to rethink, to see opportunities and to be courageous. Not just to want, but above all to do. This is also thanks to customers and partners who shared their challenges with us, did not bury their heads in the sand and maintained their joy in their own and our work.

Your six-pack is part of a wall that we built as a team, gave it a lot of paint and tore it down again. Every six-pack and every bottle in it is unique, just like you, your company and your employees. Together we are strong as a wall, but we can also tear down walls, create new things and enjoy them.

Tackling it is all of our beer. We have it in our hands.

Now, don't celebrate us. Celebrate yourself!

We are not having a Zoom aperitif. Enjoy your beer whenever you feel like it! Toast, with a sip of liquid patience. To create something with fermented courage, even if it may not be successful right away. Feel the joy of life, your health and your abilities when the tingling sensation delights your taste buds.

To you guys. Thanks!
Stay healthy.

Anja, Fabian, Cyrill, Marcel and Albi
